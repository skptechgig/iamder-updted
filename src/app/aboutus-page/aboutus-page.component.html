Our Mission

Our Mission is to revolutionize the way in which people around the world exchange knowledge/information and assist each other.

Our Story:

The inspiration came when we were exploring the school for my son to admit him to the first standard. Our son got selected into 2-3 schools, we have struggled to make a decision on which school we have to admit. We then found several parents in which their kids are studying for years. We took considerable time and tried several channels to find such parents who are happy to share their opinion about the school. We have spoken to them to collect the data points of each school and then finally decided upon a school. While doing so, we have realized that there is no system in place to connect the public to talk to each other for sharing their knowledge & information.
