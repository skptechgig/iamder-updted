Why Iamder!

  • To help the society grow together & faster by connecting the people on a problem to solution.
  • To provide complete satisfaction to help-seekers by connecting them with experts who can solve their problem(s) instantly.
  • To provide complete satisfaction to Expert by meriting him/her in return to the help provided.

Why Iamder

We discovered that people spend lot of time searching for solutions to the problems which can be solved within seconds with a single call to an expert. The world lacks a system which instantly connects help-seekers to domain experts.

We are keen on building ‘Iamder’ platform where individuals need for instant solution/answer/suggestion is fulfilled by the domain experts at the touch of a button. Our platform brings satisfaction to both sets of people: Instant help-seekers (problem is solved by an expert) & Experts (paid for consultation).

We believe an expert need not be a certified professional. Every individual in the world is an expert with his/her own skillsets. The expertise can be gained through intense practice or through experience from daily regular work.

Iamder [/’ I Am There /] is a place to search & instantly connect with people who have skills to solve your problems (Looking for help?! Expert says “I am there”).

For Experts

Almost everyone has some skill which they can offer to others if there is decent monetary benefit offered. Sometimes, it might mean simply guiding the concerned person over a 10 minutes phone call. Presently, there is no system – which connects the people having problems with people who knows solution in a real time manner. Besides money, they also want to help the society with their skills but there is no platform for same. Therefore, despite having valuable skills, they are not able to offer it to anyone and it goes wasted.

For Users

With internet become part of day to day life, all users look up on search engine whenever they face any problem / have any question. But what they get is tons of links to pages which requires one to spend time in reading those pages. While it is useful, at times the need is just to discuss with an expert someone on phone who can immediately guide over a call. There are hardly any platforms offering such options presently.
